Whether you're shipping code, managing clients, or hustling solo — Nestly fits right into your flow.

You're in the zone. Don't lose it.

A Slack message lands. A GitHub issue needs triaging. A bug report comes in. Instead of tab-switching madness, you right-click → Send to Nestly and keep coding. Puff captures the URL, the page title, and your note — all without breaking your flow.

  • Capture GitHub issues to task queue without leaving the tab
  • Let AI decompose "refactor auth module" into 6 clear subtasks
  • Generate your daily standup from git-activity summaries
  • Search tasks by natural language: "the thing about caching"

Every meeting spawns 10 new tasks.

You're in a Notion doc, a Figma file, and a Jira board simultaneously. Nestly becomes your universal capture layer — clip anything from any page, tag it, and let AI rank what needs your attention today vs. this sprint.

  • Clip user research quotes directly into task context
  • AI-ranked backlog so you always know your P1
  • Auto-generated sprint summaries for stakeholder updates
  • One-click task breakdown for large epics

Four clients. Zero project manager. You.

Nestly is your personal operations layer. Capture client feedback from emails, organise by project, and get AI-written status updates ready to send. Puff keeps tabs on everything so you can focus on the actual work.

  • Clip client emails as tasks with full context attached

  • Priority sorting across multiple client projects
  • Never lose a "quick request" buried in a long email thread

Research tabs are a blackhole. Not anymore.

You've got 40 tabs open, three assignments due, and no idea what to tackle first. Nestly captures reading tasks from any page, breaks down big essays into steps, and ranks your due dates so you're never pulling all-nighters last minute.

  • Capture research sources as tasks with URL + summary
  • Break "write dissertation chapter" into weekly milestones
  • AI-ranked study priorities based on deadlines
  • Search your notes by topic, not by when you saved them

Tickets pour in. Priorities shift. Puff adapts.

Support tickets, incident threads, escalation chains — everything moves fast in ops. Nestly gives you one surface to triage from, with AI that understands urgency and helps you communicate clearly to your team and stakeholders.

  • Triage tickets from any helpdesk tab into Nestly queue

  • Auto-written incident summaries for post-mortems
  • Daily standup covers all active incidents, nothing missed

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to enter payment info for the free plan?

Nope. Install the extension, create an account, and start using Nestly immediately. No card needed until you decide to upgrade.

Where is my data stored?

Tasks and settings are stored securely with encryption at rest and row-level access controls. Your data belongs to you — we don't sell it or train AI on it.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel from your account settings and your plan downgrades at end of billing period. No lock-in, no tricks.

Is the AI secure?

All AI requests go through a server-side proxy. Your API keys are never exposed to the browser. Rate limiting prevents abuse.
