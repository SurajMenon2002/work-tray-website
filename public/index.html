Use Cases

Built for every kind
of deep worker

Whether you're shipping code, managing clients, or hustling solo — Nestly fits right into your flow.

You're in the zone. Don't lose it.

A Slack message lands. A GitHub issue needs triaging. A bug report comes in. Instead of tab-switching madness, right-click → Send to Nestly and keep coding.

  • Capture GitHub issues to task queue without leaving the tab
  • Let AI decompose "refactor auth module" into 6 clear subtasks
  • Generate your daily standup from git-activity summaries
  • Search tasks by natural language: "the thing about caching"

Every meeting spawns 10 new tasks.

You're in a Notion doc, a Figma file, and a Jira board simultaneously. Nestly becomes your universal capture layer — clip anything from any page and let AI rank what matters now.

  • Clip user research quotes directly into task context
  • AI-ranked backlog so you always know your P1
  • Auto-generated sprint summaries for stakeholder updates
  • One-click task breakdown for large epics

Four clients. Zero project manager. You.

Nestly is your personal operations layer. Capture client feedback, organise by project, and get AI-written status updates ready to send.

  • Clip client emails as tasks with full context attached
  • Generate professional status update emails in one click
  • Priority sorting across multiple client projects
  • Never lose a "quick request" buried in a long email thread

Research tabs are a blackhole. Not anymore.

40 tabs open, three assignments due, no idea what to tackle first. Nestly ranks your due dates so you're never pulling all-nighters.

  • Capture research sources as tasks with URL + summary
  • Break "write dissertation chapter" into weekly milestones
  • AI-ranked study priorities based on deadlines
  • Search your notes by topic, not by when you saved them

Tickets pour in. Priorities shift. Puff adapts.

Support tickets, incident threads, escalation chains — everything moves fast in ops. Nestly gives you one triage surface with AI that understands urgency.

  • Triage tickets from any helpdesk tab into Nestly queue
  • AI assigns urgency scores based on context you paste
  • Auto-written incident summaries for post-mortems
  • Daily standup covers all active incidents, nothing missed

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need payment info for the free plan?

Nope. Install the extension, create an account, and start immediately. No card needed until you upgrade.

Where is my data stored?

Tasks are stored securely with encryption at rest and row-level access controls. We don't sell your data or train AI on it.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel from your account settings and your plan downgrades at end of billing period. No lock-in, no tricks.

Is the AI secure?

All AI requests go through a server-side proxy. Your API keys are never exposed to the browser.
