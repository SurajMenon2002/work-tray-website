AI Features · Pro

Superpowers for
people who ship fast

WorkTray Pro adds a suite of AI tools that learn your patterns, surface what matters, and handle the boring parts of task management — so you don't have to.

🎯

Smart Priority Suggestions

AI reads your task text, due date, and recent activity to suggest the correct P1/P2/P3 level. It explains why — and you can always override it. No black box.

✦ Pro feature
📋

Daily Standup Generator

Click once. WorkTray collects yesterday's completions and today's active tasks, then generates a paste-ready standup in your team's format. Slack, Teams, Notion — just paste.

✦ Pro feature
⊞

AI Task Breakdown

Turn vague tasks like "plan marketing campaign" into 3–5 actionable subtasks with one click. Select which ones to add to your tray. Your big scary task becomes manageable steps.

✦ Pro feature
🔍

Natural Language Search

Ask your tray anything: "What did I do last week?", "Show urgent auth bugs", "What is Sarah working on?". WorkTray understands intent, not just keywords.

✦ Pro feature
🔒
Every AI feature has an on/off switch
Don't want AI priority on your personal tasks? Turn it off. WorkTray never makes AI decisions without your permission. All toggles live in Settings.
